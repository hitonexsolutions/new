About Us

Welcome to RedCarpet, where creativity meets precision to deliver world-class event and exhibition solutions. Based in the UAE, we are your trusted partner in bringing concepts to life with seamless execution and impeccable attention to detail. With a strong foundation in event management and exhibition management, we go beyond the ordinary to offer a wide range of services tailored to meet your unique needs.

Our offerings include MEP works, ensuring your events are equipped with flawless mechanical, electrical, and plumbing systems, and in-house design and production, where every element is crafted with innovation and expertise. From branding and printing that captures your identity to audio-video solutions that create immersive experiences, we cover every aspect of your event journey.

Our capabilities extend to post-production services, delivering polished results that leave lasting impressions, furniture rentals to complement your event aesthetics, and kiosk and display counters that showcase your products with style. Additionally, our graphic solutions bring your ideas to life with compelling visuals, ensuring your brand stands out in every setting.

At RedCarpet, we don’t just plan events – we create moments that resonate and experiences that inspire. With a team of skilled professionals and a commitment to excellence, we transform your vision into reality, making every event memorable, impactful, and truly extraordinary.
